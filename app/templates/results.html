What is a Confusion Matrix? The confusion matrix is used to describe the performance of a classification model. The x-axis represents the predicted labels that our model classified. The y-axis represents the true labels that our team labeled on the testing data. Ideally, all the numbers in the left diagonal would equal 1 as these spaces represent a correct prediction.
